About Me

Hi, I am Wenzhao Xu, from China. Currently I am a Ph.D. Candidate and research assistant in Dept. of Civil and Environmental Engineering at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My research focus is using machine learning, data mining and geostatistics to analyze spatial or spatio-temporal sensor data in environmental area. I am in "Environmental Informatics and Systems Analysis" Research Group. Previously I was an intern in Dow AgroSciences in UIUC Research Park and Bosch Research and Technology Center, both focusing on data analysis. I am continuing an internship with Dow AgroSciences for Fall 2016 semester.

Attended Courses: Artifical Intelligence, Database Systems, Numerical Analysis, Statistical Learning, Cyber Physial System, Non-Linear Methods in Hydrology, Machine Learning, Machine Learning in Signal Processing, Parallel Programming, Scientific Visualization and some courses related with water resources and water quality.
